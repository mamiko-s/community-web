FAQs

When you are purchasing an item, coupons are automatically applied. If you have more than one eligible coupon, tap “Coupons” on the checkout screen to select which one you want to apply.

If your return is not in transit within the required time frame, the transaction will be completed and payment will be released to your seller. If you are having any trouble returning an item, please contact us immediately.

Sometimes tracking will stop updating while the order is still on its way to the buyer. When this happens the transaction may be cancelled before it's delivered to the buyer.

Upon receipt of the item(s), Buyer can refuse the order if it is damaged or not as described. Buyer remains responsible for the initial delivery cost. The item will be returned to the Seller immediately and Seller may be responsible for return delivery fees.
